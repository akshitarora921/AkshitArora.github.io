Scottish rock band from the town of Kilmarnock, appeared on the indie scene more than twenty years ago. Almost a million and a half sold albums, four of the seven albums - in the top of the UK Albums chart. The 'Puzzle' album of 2007 became gold in the UK.'Only Revolutions' brought them a nomination for the Barclaycard Mercury Prize. And in 2013 the album 'Opposites' came in first place in the UK charts. Soloist Simon Neal and the twin brothers Johnston play a dynamic alternative rock with a solid metal base. After the first three records, Biffy Clyro grew up, won the public's love, became the headliners of big music festivals, but remained true to the previous style. Four NME Prizes and two - the Q Awards.

A young alternative band that appeared in London from the acoustic duo Elli Roussel and Joffa Oddy. The team says that they play pop-rock, or more precisely 'rocky pop'. In 2012 their composition Leaving you got into the rotation of BBC Radio 1 and was marked by the journal NME. Two years later - the first contract with the label and the prize of the UK Festival for the best breakthrough. In 2015 - the debut studio album My Love Is Cool and the iTunes Award "Best New Band". NME nominees of the last three years, Wolf Alice won the award in 2016 as "Best Performance Group" and "Best Song". The single Moaning Lisa Smile reached the ninth line on the Billboard chart and was nominated for the Grammy in 2016. In September 2017, the second studio album Visions of a Life, work atmospheric, energetic and not smoothed, that for a guitar group only plus. Speaking at ATMF 2018 is part of a big tour in support of the album.

Miles Kane and his band seem to be a quintessential British style, almost like Union Jack. A veteran of the indie scene in his thirties - Miles Peter Kane is known for his participation in The Rascals and the loud project The Last Shadow Puppets with Alex Turner from Arctic Monkeys. In a solo project, Kane confesses his love for the 60th - a black suit and a hairstyle for the young Jagger. And this is not news - the whole Brit-pop firmly holds its musical roots. So, continuing the romantic tradition Miles, a little like the young McCartney, convincingly copes with the melodic tones.

Young indie band from Liverpool. In 2013 they released their debut single "Get Away / Good For Me", and a year later they not only opened the NME Awards tour, they were opening for The 1975, but managed to release the EP "Young Chasers" on the American label Virgin. The single was followed by the self-titled album, performances at festivals, including Glastonbury, Reading & Leeds and Boardmasters. The newly made "Liverpool Four" did not stop there and released the second album "Different Creatures" a year ago.

Молодая арт-рок группа, преемники Arcade Fire и U2, если верить газете Guardian. Солист Оскар Поллок, или попросту Lulu, ироничный Элли Бати и Дом Корделлом встретились, как и положено альтернативщикам, в римско-католической школе. Их первый альбом Youth is Only Ever Fun in Retrospect сразу попал в чарт UK Albums и дал понять — впереди стадионы. Мощный и харизматичный голос Поллока подчеркивает звенящие в воздухе гитарные рифы и напоминает интеллектуальный рок 80-х и лучшие годы Брюса Спрингстина. Sundara Karma разогревали Two Door Cinema Club, Bastille и Everything Everything, а год назад отлично выступили на большом американском фестивале South by Southwest.

Молодой музыкант из Бедфорда, балансирует между инди и утонченным соулом. Несмотря на свои 22 года, он, кажется что-то знает об этой жизни. А газета Guardian рекомендует читателям не спускать с него глаз в этом году. Дело не только в серьезных и жестких текстах и вдумчивых интонациях. Немного романтик, немного скиталец, Греннан поет баллады уверенным голосом с приятной хрипотцой.
